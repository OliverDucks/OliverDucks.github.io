My name is Oliver.

 

I am a simple duck trying to live a simple life.

It all started innocently enough - a neighborhood

kid used me to "duck" the Jeep next door.

 

This is my story.

 

 

One day, a flamingo showed up

in Jeep guy's side yard.

 

 

 

 

A couple days later, two more flamingos appeared in his back yard.

 

 

 

 

Before anyone could notice, the flamingos quickly multiplied.

 

 

 

 

If the flamingos were not bad enough,

skeletons started arriving.

 

 

 

Soon, the skeletons started riding the flamingos.

This cannot be good.

 

 

 

It became obvious the skeletons had organized and were planning something.

 

 

 

A huge flamingo arrived in the back yard.

Who or what rides this?

 

 

 

How does Jeep guy not know what is happening in his yard?

 

 

 

Went into Jeep guy's house for a PB&J sandwich.

Woke up with a headache and in a cage!

 

 

I've heard bad things about Jeep guy's monkey

This will not end well.

 

Lil' Beppo says "I am an ape, not a monkey, but this is Oliver's story."

 

 

While the crazy monkey and skeletons were distracted by the USPS carrier,

I squeezed through the bars and made a run for it.

 

 

I'm safe in this Jeep, yes?

This is where that kid wanted me, RIGHT?!?!

 

 

 

How did the crazy monkey know I am in the Jeep?

 

 

Lil'Beppo says "Once again, I am an ape, not a monkey."

 

It is coming for me.

I must escape.

 

 

Jeep guy left the back window open.

I feel sorry for the little ducks who couldn't get out.
