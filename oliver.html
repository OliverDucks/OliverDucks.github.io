My name is Oliver.

 

I am a simple duck trying to live a simple life.

It all started innocently enough - a neighborhood

kid used me to duck the Jeep next door.

 

This is my story.

 

 

One day, a flamingo showed up

in Jeep guy's side yard.

 

 

 

 

A couple days later, two more flamingos appeared in his back yard.

 

 

 

 

Before anyone noticed, the flamingos multiplied drastically.

 

 

 

 

If the flamingos were not bad enough,

skeletons started arriving.

 

 

 

 

Soon, the skeletons started riding the flamingos!

 

 

 

 

It became obvious the skeletons had organized and were planning something.

 

 

 

A huge flamingo arrived in the back yard.

Who or what rides this?

 

 

 

Even the skeletons are having problems controlling this bird.
